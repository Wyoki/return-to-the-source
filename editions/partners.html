The Partnership Journey

From initial conversations to the successful execution of our first edition, our partnerships have evolved through shared commitment and mutual respect.

Strategic Alignment

Initial partnership discussions focused on aligning organizational missions with the Return to the Source vision of cultural healing through cinema.

Resource Integration

Combining Mastercard's funding expertise, Goethe-Institut's cultural networks, Old Location Films' production capabilities, and Mucii Pictures' creative vision.

Community Engagement

Deep collaboration with local elders, historians, and community leaders to ensure authentic representation and cultural sensitivity.

Successful Execution

The inaugural edition brought together 12 filmmakers from across Africa, creating films that honor colonial history and cultural memory.
