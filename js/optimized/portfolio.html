Areas of Expertise

Key Contributions

Theoretical Framework

Developed "Oudano" as a sophisticated decolonial methodology for preserving and transmitting indigenous knowledge systems.

Research Innovation

Pioneered "Archival F(r)ictions" methodology, creating deliberate tensions between established archives and contemporary performance.

Artistic Practice

Founded Tschuku Tschuku band project, employing "sonic formation" to reinterpret historical sound archives.
