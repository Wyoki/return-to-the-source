Core Innovation

Integrating indigenous healing practices with contemporary community care to address historical trauma and foster collective wellbeing. Through a unique blend of traditional wisdom and modern therapeutic approaches, Marcella creates safe spaces for communities to process historical trauma while reconnecting with ancestral healing practices.
