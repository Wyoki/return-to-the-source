Core Innovation

Using cinema to exorcise historical trauma while crafting futures rooted in African cosmologies. Through a unique blend of archival footage, contemporary narrative, and indigenous storytelling techniques, Perivi creates films that not only document history but actively participate in the healing of collective memory.
