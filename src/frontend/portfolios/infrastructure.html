Core Innovation

Building deep Southern African relationships that honor cultural sovereignty and community wisdom. Through the Return to the Source Film Lab, Fibby creates authentic partnerships with local elders, traditional leaders, and diaspora communities, ensuring that African stories are told with cultural integrity and historical accuracy. The focus is on relationship-building that transcends borders and generations.

Key Achievements

Founded and led the groundbreaking 5-day film lab in Namibia, bringing together 12 filmmakers from across Africa with local elders and historians to create films that honor colonial history and cultural memory
