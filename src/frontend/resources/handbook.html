RTS1 Film Lab Handbook

Complete documentation of Edition 1: September 6-10, 2024, Namibia

Return to the Source: Pan-African Film Lab & Residency Programme

Dates: September 6-10, 2024

Venue: Otjisazu Guest Farm, Ovitoto; Goethe-Institut Namibia, Windhoek

Funded by: Mastercard Enablement Programme & Goethe-Institut Namibia

Supported by: Mucii Pictures & Old Location Films

Welcome Note

"It is with great pleasure that we welcome you to this groundbreaking initiative designed to connect history, cinema, and community through the powerful medium of film. Over the next five days, we will embark on a journey that delves deep into Namibia's rich yet traumatic past, exploring its colonial history and its enduring impact on contemporary life."

— Perivi Katjavivi & Fibby Kioria

Programme Schedule

Day 1: Friday, September 6 - Introduction & Memory Sites

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM: Welcome Address and introductions
10:00 AM - 1:00 PM: Site Visit: Historical Exploration at Ovitoto
2:00 PM - 3:00 PM: Incorporating Archives into Film and Research
3:30 PM - 6:00 PM: Site Visit: Historical Explorations at Otjisazu
7:00 PM - 9:30 PM: Outdoor Screening: "A Story of Bones"

Day 2: Saturday, September 7 - African Cinema and Historical Contexts

9:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Introduction to African Film Genres
10:30 AM - 11:30 AM: Masterclass: Film Screening and Discussion
11:30 AM - 1:00 PM: Masterclass: Shooting and Editing on Smartphones
2:00 PM - 3:30 PM: Masterclass: Field Recording Sound
7:00 PM - 8:30 PM: Outdoor Screening: "Under the Hanging Tree"

Day 3: Sunday, September 8 - Shooting with the Local Community

9:00 AM - 1:00 PM: Filmmaking Session: Film a location or landscape
2:00 PM - 3:00 PM: Special Address: German colonial history debates
3:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Master Class: Making "Under the Hanging Tree"
7:00 PM - 9:30 PM: Outdoor Screening: "Das Leere Grab"

Day 4: Monday, September 9 - Theoretical Discussions

9:30 AM - 11:00 AM: Interactive Panel: "Custodians of Memory?"
11:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Master Class: Making "Das leere Grab"
1:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Group Filming Activity
4:30 PM - 6:00 PM: Editing Session

Day 5: Tuesday, September 10 - Screenings and Reflections

9:00 AM - 1:00 PM: Editing Session
2:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Final Film Project Presentations
4:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Closing Remarks
