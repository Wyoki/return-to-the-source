Portfolio Overview

Reclaiming African Memory. Re-storying the Future.

Return to the Source operates on a fundamental principle: authentic African storytelling emerges not from extractive methodologies, but from deep-rooted cultural practices that honor ancestry, community, and land. We are four practitioners who have discovered that the most powerful narratives arise when theory, infrastructure, vision, and healing work in concert.
